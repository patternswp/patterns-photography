Patterns Photography is a sleek and visually captivating block-based WordPress theme designed for photographers, creative studios, and visual artists. It offers all the tools needed to build a stunning and professional online portfolio.

== Description ==

Patterns Photography is a modern and visually striking Full Site Editing (FSE) WordPress theme, ideal for photographers, creative studios, and visual storytellers looking to establish a compelling online presence. Leveraging WordPress FSE, it offers effortless customization of headers, footers, templates, and global styles within the Site Editor.

This theme includes a curated collection of pre-designed patterns and layouts built for showcasing photography portfolios, galleries, client testimonials, service details, team bios, and contact forms. Its clean, responsive design ensures your work looks professional and captivating on all devices.

Whether you're a freelance photographer, studio owner, or content creator, Patterns Photography offers a powerful platform to present your visual work with elegance and impact.
